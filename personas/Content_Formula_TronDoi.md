# Content Formula – Simpia Trọn Đời Ads

## 1. Overview
This document defines a **single, repeatable formula** that combines:
- **Emotional trigger** (Vietnam‑specific)
- **Copy structure** (PAS or HSSBC)
- **Visual layout rules** (badge, headline, sub‑headline, price block, CTA, visual cues)

The formula can be instantiated for any **Persona + P_Level** from the matrix.

## 3. Content Formula Template
```
[Badge]   <-- placed according to Visual Cue column
[Headline]   (max 7‑8 words, all‑caps, focus on benefit)
[Sub‑headline]   (4‑6 words, supportive detail)
[Price Section]   "Từ <old_price> giảm sâu còn <new_price>" – use strike‑through on <old_price>
[CTA]   (action verb + urgency, e.g., "Mua ngay – chỉ còn 2.990K")
[Tagline]   (optional, <12 words, reinforcing emotional trigger)
```
**Place‑holders** to replace when instantiating:
- `<Badge>` – e.g., `BLACK FRIDAY` or `KHÔNG GIAO HẠN`
- `<Headline>` – e.g., `SIMPIA GÓI TRỌN ĐỜI`
- `<Sub‑headline>` – e.g., `Đầu tư 1 lần – Con giỏi mãi mãi`
- `<old_price>` / `<new_price>` – numeric values
- `<CTA>` – e.g., `Mua ngay – chỉ còn 2.990K`
- `<Tagline>` – e.g., `Dành cho mọi trình độ`

## 5. Guidelines for Copywriters
1. **Pick the Persona & P_Level** – locate the row in the mapping table.
2. **Select the emotional trigger** – keep the wording aligned with Vietnamese cultural nuance (e.g., “hiếu kinh”, “tự hào”, “sợ thiệt thòi”).
3. **Choose PAS or HSSBC** – follow the column; PAS for problem‑centric messages, HSSBC for short‑form video.
4. **Fill the template** – replace placeholders, keep headline ≤ 8 words, sub‑headline ≤ 6 words.
5. **Visual consistency** – follow the Visual Cue column (badge color, font weight, CTA button color). Use the brand palette (primary #00B14F, accent #FFB400).
6. **Proofread** – ensure Vietnamese diacritics are correct; no English‑only words unless part of a brand tag.
